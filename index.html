About Me

Md. Abdullah-Al-Forhad is a Lecturer in Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at European University of Bangladesh. He pursued his bachelor's degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at East West University.

He has also worked as a Graduate teaching assistant (GTA) and Undergraduate teaching assistant (UTA) at East West University for four (4) and eight (8) months respectively.

His research interest falls into the cloud computing, internet of things (IoT), sensor device, security and privacy, and mobile computing. Specifically, his research works focus on information retrieval, and Security of IoT systems.

He has a keen interest in movies and enjoys traveling.
